Government Schemes for Electronics Industry Assistance

Central Government Schemes

  1. Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for IT Hardware:
    • This scheme provides production-linked incentives to boost domestic manufacturing of IT hardware, including laptops, tablets, servers, and other electronic products.
    • PLI pdf
  2. Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)
    • Collateral-free loans for MSMEs up to ₹2 crore.
    • Resources: CGTMSE Website
  3. · Scheme for Promotion of Manufacturing of Electronic Components and Semiconductors (SPECS):
    • This scheme aims to promote domestic manufacturing of electronic components and semiconductors.
    • Resources: SPECS pdf
  4. Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE):
    • Provides collateral-free credit guarantee to MSMEs, making it easier to secure loans from banks and financial institutions.
    • Resources: CGTMSE pdf

State Government Schemes (Delhi)

  1. Delhi Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) Facilitation Council:
    • Provides guidance and assistance to MSMEs in Delhi, including information on various government schemes and financial assistance programs.
    • Resources: DCMSME pdf
