Government Schemes for Heavy Machinery Industry Assistance

Central Government Schemes

  1. Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
    • Subsidized loans up to ₹25 lakh for micro-enterprises in the Heavy Industry sector.
    • Official Website: PMEGP Guidelines
  2. Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)
    • Collateral-free loans for MSMEs up to ₹2 crore.
    • Resources: CGTMSE Website
  3. National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC)
    • Offers various schemes for financing, marketing, and technology upgradation for MSMEs, including textile units.
    • Resources: NISC pdf

State Government Schemes (Uttar Pradesh)

  1. Uttar Pradesh Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Policy 2017:
    • This policy offers various incentives and support to MSMEs, including those in the heavy machinery industry.
    • Resources: InvestUP pdf
    • Resources: InvestUP pdf
  2. Chief Minister's Employment Generation Programme (CMEGP):
    • This scheme provides financial assistance to micro and small enterprises, including those in the manufacturing sector.
    • Resources: PMEGP pdf
  3. Other State-Specific Schemes:
    • Uttar Pradesh government may have other specific schemes for the manufacturing sector, including the heavy machinery industry.
    • UPIEPP2022 PDF
