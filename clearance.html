Types of Clearances

Comprehensive support for all major industrial clearances

Environmental Clearance

Mandatory approval from environmental authorities for industrial operations

Key Requirements:

  • Environmental Impact Assessment
  • Pollution Control Measures
  • Waste Management Plan

Estimated Processing Time: 60-90 days

Pollution Control

Certification for air, water, and noise pollution control compliance

Key Requirements:

  • Emission Control Systems
  • Water Treatment Plan
  • Noise Level Assessment

Estimated Processing Time: 30-45 days

Factory License

Operating license for manufacturing facilities

Key Requirements:

  • Building Safety Certificate
  • Fire Safety Clearance
  • Worker Safety Measures

Estimated Processing Time: 45-60 days

Industrial License

Permission for specific industrial activities and production

Key Requirements:

  • Project Report
  • Land Use Permission
  • Investment Details

Estimated Processing Time: 30-45 days

Legal Compliance

Adherence to industrial laws and regulations

Key Requirements:

  • Company Registration
  • Tax Registrations
  • Labor Law Compliance

Estimated Processing Time: 15-30 days

Power Connection

Industrial power supply and electrical safety clearance

Key Requirements:

  • Load Requirement Details
  • Electrical Safety Audit
  • Power Backup Plan

Estimated Processing Time: 30-45 days

Clearance Process

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth clearance process for your industry:

  1. Consultation: Schedule a consultation to understand your requirements and gather necessary information.
  2. Document Preparation: Prepare all required documentation as per the clearance guidelines.
  3. Submission: Submit the application to the relevant authorities.
  4. Review and Inspection: Authorities review the application and conduct site inspections if needed.
  5. Approval: Receive approval or feedback to make required adjustments.

Our team ensures guidance at every step to make the process hassle-free and efficient.
